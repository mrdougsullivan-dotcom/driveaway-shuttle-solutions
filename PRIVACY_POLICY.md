# Privacy Policy for Driveaway Shuttle Solutions

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2025

## Introduction

Doug S. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Driveaway Shuttle Solutions mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Driver Contact Information**: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, locations, and service areas of driveaway shuttle companies
- **Administrative Access**: Login credentials for administrative users

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device information and identifiers
- App usage statistics
- Error and performance logs

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Display driveaway shuttle company contact information
- Enable communication with shuttle companies via phone and email
- Allow administrators to manage driver listings
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Troubleshoot technical issues

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored securely on our servers
- We use industry-standard encryption for data transmission
- Administrative access is protected by authentication
- We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services including:
- Expo (app development platform)
- Authentication services
- Analytics services

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the information we store
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your information
- Opt out of data collection where applicable

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Data Retention

We retain driver information indefinitely or until an administrator removes it from the system. Administrative account information is retained while the account is active.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to request deletion of your personal information.

## Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:
- **Camera**: To take photos of driver contact information
- **Photo Library**: To upload screenshots of driver contact information
- **Phone**: To enable direct calling of shuttle companies
- **Email**: To enable email communication with shuttle companies

These permissions are only used for their stated purposes and can be revoked in your device settings at any time.
